Artist Biography

California based artist, Hannah Fu, has been tuning her skills in the path of digital media art. Transitioning from traditional sketchy drawings and bringing them to life in the digital world in the form of illustrations, gifs, and games. Taking inspiration from lurking in pop culture to drifting around places. Shifting between breathtaking works, simple little doodles, to unhinged creations from the pits of the abyss.
