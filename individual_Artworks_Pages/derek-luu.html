Super Random Animation Player

Created By: Derek Luu (he/him)

Artist Statement

This is an endless looping animation of a funny-looking character. Why is this character here, and what is he doing? In these randomly playing animations, you can see what this character is able to do in weird, cool, interesting ways. The only way to see what his limits are, is to watch. This character is a mascot for my ideas and interests. Inspired by short-form content like TikTok, what if I make short videos infinite and looping? As an artist I have always felt that anything creative is a good outlet and is able to encourage, teach, or humor people. It is also a symbol of my skills and my interests. The entire project should be a symbol of who and what my art is, not a magnum opus but definitely a showcase of this chapter in my life. Animated short clips are randomly put together and played with JavaScript on a website for ease of access. It will be placed on a website so that it can be displayed on multiple monitors, be easily spread and continue to be available to viewers everywhere.

Artist Biography

Derek Luu is a Vietnamese American who was born and raised in the Bay Area and has been making art since 2019. He is a digital artist who focuses on web design, illustrations, and animations. He draws inspiration from games, animated films, comics, and more. He strives to create works of art that are funny, cute, or cool to entertain friends, family, and strangers. Growing up games, animations, and other related works that are found on the internet have always been a form of escape for Derek. Being able to de-stress and take things step by step he strives to be able to create cool films, funny websites, or cute comics to offer people comfort and entertainment.
