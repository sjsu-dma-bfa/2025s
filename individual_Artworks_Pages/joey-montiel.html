Look Up

Created By: Joey Montiel (she/her)

Artist Statement

Look Up is a short film that acts as a personal journal, created as a response to events emerging in late 2024 and early 2025. Rather than having direct commentary or any definitive answer to specific and current issues, Look Up aims to convey a generalized response to them as a whole, conveying the frustrations of most of the current younger generation. This work acts as documentation of how it feels like to live in a period of uncertainty and rapid change. Inspired by the artist's frustrations and emotions of this time, this work reflects their attempt to process these issues. Additionally, the artist also acts as a voice of their generation, using the collective feelings to drive the narrative shared as a poem in the film. Look Up utilizes a mix of film, voiceover, and visuals to create a sense of transparency and emotional vulnerability. The film is not meant to give answers or solutions but rather an act of witnessing the ever-changing world - the way headlines blur into everyday life, private thoughts which reflect the unrest felt by this generation. This work serves as a reminder to 'look up' as the world around us changes. It invites viewers to not look away, but rather dissect their own feelings about current events to take notice and stay present as the world transforms.
