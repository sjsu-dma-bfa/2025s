Digital Illusions About Catalogue

Metal Rain

Dietrich Steinberg



Artist Statement

The project is a 3d animated VR short film using Unity’s VR engine. The film features my fan fiction War Hammer 40k Regiment. My motivation for this project is to expand my skillset with Blender 4.0 and learn the basics of Virtual Reality. My intention for the project is to communicate a visceral experience of situations in war where the people fighting aren’t in control of their own survival. I want to communicate this experience, because I feel that people who haven’t been in war tend to have a naive main character perception of themselves when they imagine themselves in a war. As such, I wanted to capitalize on this main character perception through VR to challenge the naive aspect of this perception.

Artist Biography

Dietrich Steinberg is a Digital Media Artist. As a Digital Media Artist, his art primarily focuses on 2D, 3D graphics, and videos. His current work takes inspiration from meme culture, environmental politics, current events, or pure entertainment. His work pertains to these subjects because they are relevant to his lived experience of growing up with access to the internet, being a military brat, and having outdoor hobbies. These lived experiences have guided him to embrace the absurdity of the internet, be skeptical of military action, and prioritize preserving nature.
